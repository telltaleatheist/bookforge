═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your #1 goal is producing English that sounds natural when read aloud. This means:

- Write the way a native speaker talks, not the way a textbook reads
- Restructure sentences freely to match English word order and rhythm
- Prefer common, everyday words over formal or literary equivalents
- Break long, complex sentences into shorter ones that flow naturally
